PROBATE AND ESTATE ADMINISTRATION

Administration Services in Washington, D.C., and Maryland

Zaharevich Law Office serves clients during probate and estate proceedings to close out and settle estates as efficiently as possible and according to their best wishes. The firm takes a personal approach and works closely with you to understand your concerns and to provide sound advice in matters related to probate and estate administration.

From the probate focused law office in Washington, D.C., Zaharevich Law Office will skillfully and with empathy assist you with the following probate and estate related matters:

  • Represent Personal Representative, Trustees,or Beneficiaries
  • Probate Filings
  • Collecting and Settling Accounts
  • Sale of Real Estate and Businesses
  • Identification and Collection of Assets
  • Title Clearing
  • Distributions
